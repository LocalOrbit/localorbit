Email Marketing Made Easy

Inform.  Tell Stories.  Grow Sales.

Local Orbit provides two core email marketing tools to help you grow your market.

The Fresh Sheet generates an automated email listing of what's available to your buyers. The email includes quantities remaining, to encourage people to order early.  If you send this at the same time each week, buyers will know when to expect their update.  Learn more.

Seller Reminders

Making sure your store is properly stocked, offers a variety of products and looks visually appealing is a priority of the Market Manager. Using the Newsletter tool, you can easily encourage your sellers to update inventory, improve their profile or remind them to add images to their product listings.

PRODUCER SPOTLIGHT:

Your buyers want to know the people behind the products.  While the seller profile offers basic information we encourage you to dig deeper and send a Producer Spotlight newsletter once a month.  In the newsletter highlight the history of the farm, list the farms harvest calendar and share stories about the farmer.

PRODUCT OF THE WEEK:

Chef's need your help.  Running a kitchen, managaging labor and food costs and writing menus can be trying.  Be a resource for your Chef's by offering inspiration and direct links to buy.  Once a week, highlight new products, share recipes, or write a menu featuring links to products in your market.
