Local Orbit powers your back office with two email marketing tools: 
The Fresh Sheet lets your customers know what's available, in real time.  
The Newsletter can be either informational or promotional, enabling you to communicate efficiently with sellers and buyers.

The Fresh Sheet

The Fresh Sheet generates an automated email listing of what's available to your buyers.  The email includes quantities remaining to encourage people to order early. Send the Fresh Sheet at the same time each week so buyers will know when to expect their update. Learn more [RAGAN ADD LINK]

Inform

Seller Reminders

A better buyer experience leads to better sales. Maintaining a diverse inventory, with photos and seller profiles, engages buyers and generates sales. Use the Newsletter feature in your dashboard to encourage sellers to update their inventories, enhance their profiles and add images to product listings.

Educate

Producer Spotlight

Your buyers want to know who producers their products.  While the seller profile offers basic information, we encourage you to dig deeper and send a Producer Spotlight newsletter once a month.  You can highlight the history of the farm, list the farm's harvest calendar and share interesting tidbits about the farmer or producer.  

Tell a Good Story

Product of the Week

Chefs can always use extra help.  Running a kitchen, managaging labor and food costs, and writing menus are time consuming.  Be a resource for your chefs by offering inspiration and links to featured products. Send weekly highlights of new products, share recipes, or let them know what will be in season in the coming weeks.
