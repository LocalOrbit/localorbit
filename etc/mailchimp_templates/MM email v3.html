Local Orbit has equipped you with several different email marketing tools to help grow your store.

Use the FRESH SHEET on a weekly basis to communicate your store's current product offering to your buyers.  Learn more here.

Use the NEWSLETTER as your main communication tool for both your buyers and sellers.  Learn more here.  And check out some of our newsletter topic recommendations:




SELLER REMINDERS:

Making sure your store is properly stocked, offers a variety of products and looks visually appealing should be a priority of the Market Manager. Using the NEWSLETTER tool, you can easily encourage your sellers to update their inventory, improve their profile or remind them to add images to their product listings.
KEY FOCUS: Improving knowledge

PRODUCER SPOTLIGHT:

Your buyers want to know who producers their products.  While the seller profile offers basic information we encourage you to dig deeper and send a Producer Spotlight newsletter once a month.  In the newsletter you can highlight the history of the farm, list the farms harvest calendar and share interesting tidbits about the farmer or producer.  
KEY FOCUS: Improving relationships.

PRODUCT OF THE WEEK:

Chef's are always researching new and innovative ways to improve their menu and offerings.  Once a month, share one of your new products that just came in season and offer sample recipes or menu's for the buyer to use as inspiration.  
KEY FOCUS: Improving sales.
