Improve your product listings

Having a complete product listing is the first step to increasing sales.  Part of the draw to local food is the transparency in the product.  Chefs want their customers to know they have done their due diligence in selecting the finest and healthiest ingredients for their meals.  In order for them to guarantee this, the Chef must have the information at his fingertips.  

A complete listing includes: an accurate (and stunning) image, a description of how the product was produced, details of the farmer and the location of the farm.  All of this information can be inputted in the product detail.  And if you need help sprucing up your image, may we suggest PicMonkey.

Exceed your buyers expectations

If you are running a cooperative or buying club there are some easy ways to enhance your member experience.  Being part of a club should be more than just receiving group discounts.  It should be about friendship, health, good food, and discounts.  Here are some of our suggestions on ways to encourage just that.

Start a loyalty program.  Offer your members special discounts or prizes once they've reached a certain number in sales.  Think grocery store loyalty programs.
Host a supper club.  Once a month, invite your members out for a potluck or catered event to enourage comraderie.  Use this time to share news about new products or offers.
